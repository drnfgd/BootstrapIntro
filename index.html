ABM

ABM stands for "Accountancy, Business, and Management." It's a strand in the senior high school curriculum in some educational systems, particularly in the Philippines. The ABM strand is designed to provide students with knowledge and skills related to business, finance, entrepreneurship, and management, preparing them for careers in fields such as accounting, finance, marketing, human resources, and business management. Students taking this strand typically study subjects like business math, business ethics, economics, finance, and organizational management.

STEM

STEM stands for "Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics." It's another strand in senior high school education, particularly in the Philippines and some other educational systems. The STEM strand is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Students in the STEM strand typically study subjects such as physics, chemistry, biology, calculus, computer science, robotics, and engineering principles. The goal of the STEM strand is to prepare students for further education and careers in STEM-related fields such as engineering, medicine, computer science, and research.

HUMSS

HUMSS stands for "Humanities and Social Sciences." It's one of the academic strands offered in senior high school education, particularly in the Philippines. The HUMSS strand is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of human behavior, society, culture, politics, and history, as well as skills in critical thinking, research, communication, and analysis. Students in the HUMSS strand typically study subjects such as sociology, psychology, anthropology, philosophy, history, literature, economics, political science, and communication. The goal of the HUMSS strand is to prepare students for careers in fields such as education, journalism, law, social work, public administration, and the humanities, as well as for further education in related disciplines at the college or university level.

ARTS AND DESIGN

Students in the Arts and Design strand typically study subjects such as visual arts, performing arts, media arts, graphic design, fashion design, industrial design, and other related disciplines. The curriculum often includes both theoretical and practical components, allowing students to develop their artistic skills, creativity, critical thinking, and aesthetic sensibilities. This strand aims to prepare students for further education and careers in the arts, design, and creative industries. It provides opportunities for students to explore and specialize in different areas of arts and design, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and portfolio necessary for pursuing higher education in fine arts, design schools, or related fields, as well as for pursuing careers as professional artists, designers, performers, or creative professionals.
